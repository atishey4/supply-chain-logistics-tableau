## Overview
This Tableau project monitors logistics performance and inventory efficiency to help operations teams reduce delays, control costs, and improve service levels.

## Sample Dataset (key fields)
- Order ID, Shipment Date, Delivery Date, Month, Quarter, Year
- Region, Warehouse Location, Supplier Name
- Product Category, Product Name
- Inventory Level, Reorder Level, Units Ordered, Units Delivered
- Delivery Status, Delivery Delay (Days)
- Transportation Cost, Inventory Holding Cost
- Stockout Status, Demand Forecast, Actual Demand
- Inventory Turnover Ratio

## KPIs & Calculations
- Total Orders, On-Time Delivery %
- Average Delivery Delay, Delay %
- Inventory Turnover Ratio, Stockout Rate %
- Total Transportation Cost, Total Inventory Cost
- Forecast Accuracy %, Supplier Performance Score

## Dashboard Views
- KPI cards for Delivery, Cost, Inventory
- Supplier Performance (bar)
- Inventory by Product Category (column)
- Monthly Delivery Trend (line)
- Delivery Status Distribution (donut)
- Warehouse vs Delivery Delay (heatmap)
- Region-wise Logistics Performance (map)
- Inventory Level vs Demand (scatter) and inventory analysis table with filters

## Insights Enabled
Detect delay patterns, supplier bottlenecks, stockout risks, and cost drivers to optimize logistics and inventory policies.
